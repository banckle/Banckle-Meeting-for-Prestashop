Prestashop Banckle Meeting module installation instructions:

1. Go to your PrestaShop’s back end (admin panel) and log in.
2. Now click on Modules tab.
3. Click on Add a module from my computer, then browse Banckle Meeting zip file and click on Upload this module.
4. Click on Other Modules and it will expand up. Now click on Install button .
5. After that click on Configure.
